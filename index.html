About

Nick Mancuso a 45 year Hollywood and International acting veteran, having more than 300 film, TV and theatre credits to his name, brings an unparalleled wealth of knowledge and experience in offering a comprehensive six week workshop entitled "Acting is for Everyone".

Using his proprietarily developed and time-tested T.O.P. System, Nick gives students an in depth introspective of acting across the millennia providing the necessary tools, insights and techniques to equip the aspiring professional film and/or television actor. Drawing from an extensive repertoire of essential skills, Nick’s T.O.P. system acts as a guiding compass showing students how to identify, address and solve the myriad of problems confronting today’s actors.

Nick’s award winning accomplishments as an actor, writer and producer provides the serious and diligent student with invaluable advantage and methodology to achieve acting success.

Workshop

Nick Mancuso a 45 year Hollywood and International acting veteran, having more than 300 film, TV and theatre credits to his name, brings an unparalleled wealth of knowledge and experience in offering a comprehensive six week workshop entitled "Acting is for Everyone".

Nick Mancuso will hold his TOP System of acting Workshop Series at 6 St. Joseph House. Nick Mancuso supports and has been inspired by this creative resource centre, which provides people who are taking a step up from livelihood challenges, with a community of people ready to help others and connects them to a ‘home’.

6 St. Joseph House is a project of the Seeds of Hope Foundation and provides A Practical and Creative Approach to Engaging Citizens in More Compassionate Responses to the Challenges of Livelihood and Homelessness

Many of its activities have the potential to serve as a model for outreach programs at other neighbourhood centres.

Located in downtown Toronto near Yonge & Wellesley St., 6 St Joseph House is a creative resource centre where everyone, no matter what their circumstances, has an opportunity to contribute their talents, feel a sense of belonging, and receive support from one another.
